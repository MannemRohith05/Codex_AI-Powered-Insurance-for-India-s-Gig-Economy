SLIDE 5 — HOW PARAMETRIC INSURANCE WORKS
Heading: What Is Parametric Insurance?
Content:
- Traditional insurance: Prove your loss → assessor visits → weeks of waiting
- Parametric insurance: A pre-agreed trigger condition is met → payout is automatic
- GigShield triggers:
  * Rainfall > 50mm in 24 hours
  * Temperature > 40°C (heatwave)
  * Air Quality Index > 300 (Hazardous)
  * Active flood OR cyclone alert in the worker's zone
- Data comes from OpenWeatherMap API, refreshed every 30 minutes
- No human bias. No delays. No paperwork.

Callout: "Parametric = faster, fairer, fraud-resistant"

SLIDE 6 — THE AI ENGINE
Heading: Intelligence Built Into Every Decision
Content:
Three AI models powering GigShield:

Model 1 — Risk Scoring (XGBoost)
- Computes a 0–100 risk score per worker per week
- Uses 8 features: rainfall, temperature, AQI, flood index, platform type, income, work hours, seniority
- Determines premium tier: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH

Model 2 — Fraud Detection (LightGBM)
- Binary classifier: Legitimate Claim vs. Fraudulent Claim
- Checks GPS zone match, claim frequency, weather confirmation, photo evidence
- Precision target: >92% | Recall: >78%

Model 3 — Anomaly Detection (Isolation Forest)
- Unsupervised detection of novel fraud patterns not seen in training data
- Flags sudden tier upgrades before storms, unusually high claim ratios vs peers

Decision Gate:
Fraud score < 0.3 + No anomaly flag → AUTO_APPROVE → UPI Payout
Fraud score > 0.3 OR anomaly flag → MANUAL REVIEW (T+2 days)
